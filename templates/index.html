🌍 Our Purpose

ReCircle is on a mission to reduce waste, foster kindness, and spark change.

How ReCircle Works

1. Become a Kindler 🔥

Create an account to join the ReCircle community of Kindlers—people who spark joy by sharing what they have.

2. Send or Catch a Spark ✨

Got extra food or useful items? Post them as Sparks. Need something? Catch a Spark shared by someone nearby.

3. Earn Spark Points 💎

Giving feels good—and earns you points! Redeem Spark Points for exclusive rewards, or just enjoy the good vibes.

🌟 Lingo Decoder

Kindler: That’s you! A member of ReCircle who sparks change by giving or receiving.

Spark: Any item shared on ReCircle—could be a snack, a shirt, or even a smile in a bag!

Spark Points: You earn these when you send Sparks. Use them to unlock rewards or perks.
